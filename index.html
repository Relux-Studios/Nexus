Welcome to Nexus

Nexus is a galaxy-themed Discord server where you can hang out, chat, and connect with others. Discussions are open-ended and not restricted to space-related topics, making it a great place for all kinds of conversations.

We have a structured role system, with galaxy-themed ranks. However, there are no custom rank progressions. Our staff team, known as *Nexus Kings* (head staff) and Moderators, ensure a fair and fun environment.

Join us today and be part of an engaging community!
